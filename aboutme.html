Information About Who I Am

Thank you for coming to my website!

First and foremost, I am a member of the First Baptist Church of Aurora, IN. At church I volunteer in many capacities including running the computer for Sunday worship services when I am able to and helping with Operation Christmas Child. I have a very supporting family that includes my mother, father, and brother. My brother is the one and only Pulzer, also known as Josh Pohle. I am also a college student at Purdue studying to become an electrical engineer. I am a Jac-Cen-Del graduate. During my time at Jac-Cen-Del, I ran Cross Country and Track (800m, 1600m, and 3200m). I also participated in Rube Goldberg with the Ripley County Engineering Academy. We placed 2nd place at nationals in 2018 and 3rd at nationals in 2019. We were a qualifying team for nationals in 2020, but because of the pandemic, we were unable to compete. I also was part of the Jac-Cen-Del Schools Livestreaming crew where we would livestream the various events at the school.
You can contact me at sethpohle@sethpohle.com. I appreciate you looking at my blogs and I hope you enjoy them.
