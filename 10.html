Happy New Years Eve - Last Post of 2020

December 31, 2020

In a year that has one surprise after another (most of them not favorable), I think it is more important than ever to look forward to making the next year a bit better. Going into this next year, I have more drive than I ever have had to make sure that I keep my New Year's resolutions and make sure that I can keep them as easily as possible. With the COVID-19 vaccine out and slowly reaching everyone in society, I am optimistic in the idea that it will help bring the pandemic to a close so that out lives can become a little more normal. I think there should be more optimism in this country right now for the next year than there has been in a really long time. Hopefully this New Year will be a good one to all of you. See you next year!
