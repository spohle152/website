First Blog Post

November 15, 2020

Hello. My name is Seth Pohle and I am a Jac-Cen-Del 2020 Graduate and have made the decision to study Electrical Engineering at Purdue University. I plan on using this blog to help me express my thoughts about what is going on in my life (as well as to spend my free time more productively).
The most important thing in my life is my belief in Jesus Christ. I am a member of the First Baptist Church of Aurora. I strive to follow Jesus in everything that I do. I am going to use this blog to help express my feelings about religion along with many other tings in my life.
Throughout my years in high school, I have the privilege of participating in many extra-curricular activities such as Rube Goldberg, Drama Club, Live Streaming, and many more. You can see a more complete list in the video on my YouTube channel (linked on the homepage) named "High School Memories"
I don't post on my YouTube channel too much, but I will occasionally post a video update. I will probably post here pretty much randomly. Whenever there is something that I want to talk about, I will probably post that here.
The main goal of this post is to let those reading my posts know a little more about me. I hope to post more and I hope you enjoy reading what I have to say.
Thanks,
